Wolf Worrior Diplomacy

November 25 2021

Wolf Warrior Diplomacy describes an aggresive style of diplomacy adopted by Chinese diplomates in the 21th century under Chinese leader Xi jinping's administration. The term was coined from a Rambo-style Chinese action film wolf worrier 2. This style contrasts to prior Chinese diplomatics practices that were maximized as "Taoguange Yanghuai" means "cover light and nurture in the dark, or keep a low profile" by Deng Xioping, which had emphasized the avoidence of controvercy and the use of cooperative rhetoric.
